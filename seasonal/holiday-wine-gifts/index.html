Who Is a Wine Club Gift Membership For?

The short answer: almost anyone who enjoys wine. But here are the specific personas who will love this gift most:

  • The wine lover who has everything: They have a full cellar and don't need another bottle. What they don't have is a sommelier choosing for them and unlimited self-pour access to 180+ wines. The experience is the gift.
  • The curious beginner: Someone who wants to learn more about wine but doesn't know where to start. The self-pour wall is the perfect educational environment — 1oz pours of dozens of varieties and regions, at their own pace, 7 days a week.
  • The Delray Beach local: A neighbor, friend, or colleague who lives in or visits South Florida regularly. The Wine Room is their new weekly ritual waiting to happen.
  • The couple or pair: Wine Club membership is for one person, but the experience is enjoyed together. Give a membership and they'll bring you along for the pickup parties and tasting nights.
  • The corporate recipient: A client or colleague who deserves something more meaningful than a bottle. A Wine Room membership says "I value our relationship enough to give you an experience, not just a thing."
