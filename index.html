How It Works

The Wine Card
Experience

Your membership includes a monthly top-up on your personal Enomatic card. Step up to any of our 26 state-of-the-art Italian wine preservation machines, insert your card, and pour. From bold Napa Cabernets to rare French Burgundies — every one of our 180+ selections is available to you, every visit, all week long.

  • Self-pour at your own pace — taste before you commit to a bottle
  • Enomatic preservation keeps every wine perfect for 30+ days
  • Three pour sizes: taste (1oz), half-glass (2.5oz), full glass (5oz)
  • Selection refreshed regularly with new arrivals and seasonal picks
