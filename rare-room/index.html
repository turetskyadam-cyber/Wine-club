The Rare Room is a members-only section of The Wine Room in Delray Beach, FL, housing over 2,400 rare, allocated, and collectible wine bottles. It includes single-vineyard Burgundies, aged Barolos, cult Napa Cabernets, Grower Champagnes, and ultra-limited releases from boutique producers worldwide — all curated and hand-selected by our sommelier.
Allocated wines are produced in such small quantities that they're distributed by the winery only to a select list of buyers — often restaurants, retailers, and private collectors with established relationships. They're rarely available in retail stores and never on typical wine shelves. The Rare Room gives members access to these bottles at member pricing. See our guide to allocated wines →
