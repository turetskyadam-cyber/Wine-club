Join for $29/Month How It Works

The Wine Room Wine Club is Delray Beach, Florida's premier wine membership program, operated by The Wine Room, Kitchen & Bar — the world record holder for the largest wine-on-tap experience since 2019. For $29 per month, members receive unlimited access to 180+ self-pour wines through 26 Italian Enomatic machines, one hand-selected bottle per month, a dollar-for-dollar wine card match, exclusive entry to The Rare Room (2,400+ allocated and rare bottles), and priority access to monthly pickup party events, wine dinners, and private tastings. There is no long-term commitment and memberships can be cancelled anytime.
