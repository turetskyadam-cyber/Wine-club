A Wine Club gift membership at The Wine Room in Delray Beach, FL is one of the most memorable and experiential wine gifts in South Florida. Rather than a single bottle, you're gifting an ongoing experience: access to 180+ self-pour wines through 26 Enomatic machines, one sommelier-selected bottle per month, a dollar-for-dollar wine card match, entry to The Rare Room's 2,400+ allocated bottle collection, and invitations to monthly events and wine dinners. Gift memberships are available in 1-month, 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month durations — all with no obligation for the recipient to continue afterward.
