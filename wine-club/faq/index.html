How the Wine Club Works

The Wine Room Wine Club ($29/month) includes: 1 hand-selected bottle per month, dollar-for-dollar wine card matching on 180+ self-pour wines (no cap), 15% off 6-bottle purchases (20% off 12 bottles), member pricing on 400+ retail labels, exclusive Rare Room access (2,400+ allocated bottles), and priority invitations to wine dinners, the monthly pickup party, and private tastings.
Load any amount onto your personal Enomatic card and we match it dollar for dollar — instantly, no cap, no minimum. Load $50, get $100 to spend. Load $200, spend $400. Use it at any of our 26 self-pour machines for 1oz, 2.5oz, or 5oz pours of 180+ wines, 7 days a week.
Enomatic machines are Italian-engineered, precision self-pour wine dispensers that preserve wine using argon gas to prevent oxidation. Each machine holds multiple bottles and allows guests to pour precise quantities — 1oz, 2.5oz, or 5oz — using a rechargeable card. The Wine Room has 26 machines hosting 180+ wine selections, making it the world's largest such installation. Learn more about Enomatic machines →
All 180+ wines on the self-pour wall are accessible with your wine card, 7 days a week. That's up to 180 unique 1oz tastes per card load if you want to explore the entire wall. Your wine card balance carries over visit to visit until it's spent — so there's no pressure to rush.
The 180+ wines rotate seasonally and include Old World and New World selections — Napa Valley Cabernet Sauvignon, French Burgundy and Rhône, Italian Barolo and Pinot Grigio, Spanish Rioja, Argentine Malbec, and more. New arrivals are added monthly based on seasonality, member feedback, and sommelier selections. Red, white, rosé, and sparkling options are all available.

The Rare Room

The Rare Room is a members-only section of The Wine Room housing 2,400+ rare and allocated wine bottles — including single-vineyard Burgundies, aged Barolos, cult Napa Cabernets, Grower Champagnes, and ultra-rare releases as notable as Romanée-Conti. Learn more about The Rare Room →
The Rare Room is primarily a Wine Club member benefit. Members receive exclusive access and member pricing. Limited walk-in access may be available for serious collectors by direct inquiry. The most comprehensive access requires a $29/month Wine Club membership.
